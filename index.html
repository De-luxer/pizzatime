PLANT CATALOG

Frangipani

Frangipani enchants with it's delicate flowers that smell wonderful

Bougainvillea

Has bright colors of different shades. It grows not only in the tropics, but also in any warm climate

Palm

A popular and most importantly beautiful tropical tree that grows up to 20 m will be an excellent decoration for your backyard

Аcacia

Each tree has a sea of flowers. And all kinds of colors are found: pink, red, white, yellow, lilac

Heliconia

Heliconia can be painted in shades of red, orange, yellow and green and reach 4.5 m in height.

Bamboo

Bamboo grows very quickly and is very tough, making it an ideal candidate to plant in your backyard.

Jasminum

The buds are painted in a pale pink color; after opening, the star-shaped white flowers attract attention.

Passiflora Incarnata

A fancifully shaped flower that will stand out from other flowers and become the highlight of your garden.

Orchid

There are a lot of orchids, only in the Amazon forests there are more than 8000 species with flowers of all possible shapes and colors.

Protea

Proteus flowers have many inflorescence shapes: giant dishwashing brushes, and pompons, pubescent with stamens.

Nepenthes Attenboroughii

This plant is shaped like a jug, and inside it has a sweet syrup that is extremely dangerous for small birds, insects and rodents.

Euphorbia

It is strongly branched with erect branches. On the edge of the branches there are tubercles, from which light green leaves grow.
